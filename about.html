ABOUT US

Our mission is to provide organic certified products to more families in Australia at a reasonable cost. The goal is to let more Australians take an organic path. It is hoped that the overall business objective in the next three years is to increase sales, improve brand awareness, develop a wider range of products and expand to overseas markets, most likely Hong Kong, Japan and Singapore.

The business was established in 2010 to cater for a demand for Australian native food products.

The age range of Australian consumers buying products is between 35 and 65. Most consumers are health conscious, care about protecting the environment and have an average annual income of $80,000.
